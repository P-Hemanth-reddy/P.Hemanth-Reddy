Movie Review

Movie Name:BRO

Director:Samuthirakani

Released date:28 July 2023

Rating: 4 Out of 5

From Actors Pawan kalyan

Highlights:

  • The movie is about Mark (Sai Dharam Tej), a successful businessman who is always rushing through life. He is constantly on the go, and he never seems to have enough time for anything. One day, he meets Titan (Pawan Kalyan), a mysterious figure who claims to be the God of Time. Titan tells Mark that he needs to slow down and enjoy life more, or else he will regret it later.
  • Undoubtedly, thaman is yet another highlight of this film. Both his music and background score elevate the film to another level.

Drawbacks:

  • The movie is a bit predictable at times. The audience can easily guess what is going to happen next.
  • The movie's message about the importance of living in the present is a bit heavy-handed at times.
  • The movie's special effects are not always up to par. Some of the scenes look fake or CGI-heavy.

Finally,On the whole, BRO has some very interesting moments throughout and will impress the younger generation.
